Space Characters

Unicode spaces:

Unicode has many different space characters:

Character name: Hex Dec HTML entity1 Example
Backspace: U+0008 8 foo,bar,baz,qux,quux
Character tabulation U+0009 9 &Tab; foo,bar,baz,qux,quux
Space: U+0020 32 foo, bar, baz, qux, quux
No-break space: U+00a0 160 &nbsp; foo, bar, baz, qux, quux
En space: U+2002 8194 &ensp; foo, bar, baz, qux, quux
Em space: U+2003 8195 &emsp; foo, bar, baz, qux, quux
Three-per-em space: U+2004 8196 &emsp13; foo, bar, baz, qux, quux
Four-per-em space: U+2005 8197 &emsp14; foo, bar, baz, qux, quux
Six-per-em space: U+2006 8198 foo, bar, baz, qux, quux
Figure space: U+2007 8199 &numsp; foo, bar, baz, qux, quux
Punctuation space: U+2008 8200 &puncsp; foo, bar, baz, qux, quux
Thin space: U+2009 8201 &thinsp; foo, bar, baz, qux, quux
Hair space: U+200a 8202 &hairsp; foo, bar, baz, qux, quux
Zero width space: U+200b 8203 &ZeroWidthSpace; foo,​bar,​baz,​qux,​quux
Narrow no-break space: U+202f 8239 foo, bar, baz, qux, quux
Medium mathematical space: U+205f 8287 &MediumSpace; foo, bar, baz, qux, quux
ideographic space: U+3000 12288 foo,　bar,　baz,　qux,　quux

1. you can also use &#xnnnn; (hex) or &#nnnn; (dec) for any unicode character. E.g. &#x2002; or &#8194; for an en space.
