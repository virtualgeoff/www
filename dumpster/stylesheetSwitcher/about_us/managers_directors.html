Managers & Directors

NB: some titles differ from 'who' page.

Ian Jones

Chief Executive Officer

Ian is a business development manager with business start-up experience in many sectors.

Most recently he was CEO of a biotechnology company in Europe during its initial 2 year start-up phase. This project involved the privatisation and commercialisation of two biotechnology branches from within a University to create a commercial operation and an intellectual property pipeline.

In 1997 Ian was a co-founder of Ag-Gene Ltd, now ASX listed biotechnology company Benitec Ltd. He remains a participant in biotech business in Australia and New Zealand.

From 1994 to 1997 he was active in business development in Asia, mainly Singapore and China.

During the early 1990s, Ian was involved in business development in the franchise sector, where he owned and operated two successful franchises and was a franchisor.

From 1978 to 1988, Ian was in the Commonwealth Public Service, where he held senior management positions in Canberra and in the Queensland State Office.

 

Graham Miskell

Director, Business Development

Graham is a business and economic development consultant.

He spent over 38 years in most sectors of the banking industry, including time as Executive Director of Hambros Australia (1980 - 1982), responsible for all Hambros Group business development, lending and property services.

For 9 years until 1991 he served as a Director of Hambros Australia. He was also the Hambros Queensland State Director and Group Director of Project Finance and Property Services.

Graham has a lifetime of experience in the sector. He has been a Branch Manager for the Bank of New South Wales, and the Business Development Manager for listed finance company Australian Finance and Securities Ltd.

Graham has most recently been occupied as an economic development consultant assisting a number of Local Government authorities.

In 1999, he served on the Western Australian Private Enterprise Panel of the WA Department of Primary Industries, providing finance advice on the development of rural export industries.

His great knowledge of banking and finance was a key factor in the invitation to join the Board of FTA.

 

William Walder

Director, Sales

Bill has 40 years experience in the finance industry, many of those years spent with major banking and finance organisations in Australia.

He has over 20 years directly involved in the Current Asset Finance side of the industry.

He brings with him a long list of valuable contacts in industry and Government, both in Queensland and interstate.

His knowledge, depth of experience and his track record of getting things done will be big contributors to the success of this business venture.

 

Gary Tolliday

Commercial Finance Manager

Gary has been in the banking and finance sector since 1963 when he commenced with the Commonwealth Bank in Victoria.

He subsequently worked for Commercial & General Acceptance Limited until 1986, when he started his own finance broking business in Ballarat.

Gary operated as a highly successful and independent finance broker for 16 years until he sold his business in 2002 to commence work with FTA.

He has a strong business and marketing background, and extensive experience in structuring complex commercial financing and leasing packages.

Gary was a high profile participant in sports, business and service organisations in the central Victorian region for many years.
