Home

Finance Technology Australia provides financial and business management services to Australian small and medium sized enterprises. It was established in Brisbane in 2002 by a team of experienced bankers, financiers and business entrepreneurs.

Finance Technology Australia holds an Australian Financial Services Licence from the Australian Securities and Investment Commission, and a Builders License from the Queensland Building Services Authority.

FTA has business activities in:-

  • Property Development
  • Accounts Receivable Financing
  • Invoice Discounting
  • Financial and Business Management Services
  • Asset Securitisation Trust Services
  • IT Professional Services (ITPS)
  • Partco Australia
