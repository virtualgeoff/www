FTA offers two unique current asset financing services to SMEs.

Accounts Receivable Financing

An FTA financing innovation, this is similar to factoring with several unique characteristics that make it ideal for small companies seeking a flexible, low cost ledger financing facility.

The facility operates on an overall agreed ledger balance of available funds, rather than on an invoice by invoice offer/purchase agreement between the client company and FTA. This substantially reduces the administration required to run a debtor's ledger financing facility.

The facility is simple, flexible and enables a client to draw against the available balance at any time.

FTA can, for an additional fee, provide value added services to your business, for example, monthly or weekly business performance measures against certain key performance indicators (such as stock turn ratios, GP and NP percentages and so on).

If you could benefit from an acceleration of your cashflow, but don't like the thought of factoring, then FTA's Accounts Receivable Financing facility may be just want you need. Contact us for more information.

Government Invoice Discounting

FTA's unique InvoiceAdvance facility frees up cash flow by paying up to 95% of Government invoices direct into your bank account within 48 hours of approval.

The InvoiceAdvance facility operates with no financial information, personal guarantees or mortgages.

The facility is completely flexible, and allows you to choose which invoices to discount, and when. There is no complicated agreement that locks you into compulsory discounting arrangements.

Simple documentation, low cost, no hassles and no standby or hidden fees.

With FTA's InvoiceAdvance facility, you will never have to wait up to 60 days or sometimes more to be paid by Government.

If you do business with Government and would like a flexible, optional facility to accelerate your cashflow, contact us today.
