Asset Securitisation Trust Services

FTA established a securitisation Master Trust structure in 2002/2003 to provide large scale financing secured by quality assets.

The Trustee for the Master Trust operations is JP Morgan Trust Australia Limited, part of the JP Morgan Chase Bank group.

The processes for the management of the Trust transactions are the subject of an audit by Pricewaterhouse Coopers.

The first FTA Trust was established in January 2003 to finance Government receivables. A total of $1.25million in short term debentures was issued by the Trust.

FTA is establishing other Trusts to finance property development, and other asset classes capable of being securitised.

Opportunities are available from time to time for wholesale (sophisticated) investors to subscribe for securities in the form of short term debentures.

The offer of finance is regulated by FTA's AFS Licence.

If you are a sophisticated investor seeking additional investment opportunities, contact us for the current Offer Document.
