IT Professional Services (ITPS)

FTA provides innovative financial solutions to Australian SMEs.

An important part of any business solution is to have a reliable, cost-effective IT framework. This means an IT system that allows managers to monitor their critical financial and business performance ratios from any location, and at any time.

Because IT Professional Services is part of a finance and business services company, our IT solutions are designed by business people for business people. When you come to us, you won't get a team of computer boffins creating systems you don't understand and your staff can't use.

If you already have an IT network and need better support and maintenance, our Senior Systems Engineer can look after you on a casual or contract basis. We can promise you'll get the service when you want it, and at very competitive rates.

Our IT Professional Services include:

  • Service and maintenance of your existing networks
  • Review of your current IT infrastructure
  • Development of new IT solutions for better business performance

For a confidential, no obligation assessment of your IT needs, contact our Senior Systems Engineer Scott Ferguson today on support@fta-au.com.
