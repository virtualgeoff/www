Current Angle of the Earth's Axis wrt Sun

i.e., The component of the tilt of the Earth's axis in the direction of the Sun. It is 0° at the Equinoxes and ±23.44° at the Solstices. How to calculate the angle for other days?

See: http://answers.yahoo.com/question/index?qid=20071106110544AAmw5Tj

The formula is:
X = today - March equinox / 365
Angle = 23.44 * sin(X*360 degrees)
This is good enough for plus or minus 1 day (about).
